# Flight Day/Night Calculator – Privacy Policy

Flight Day/Night Calculator does not collect, use, store, or share any personal data.

All calculations, searches, and lookups are performed entirely on-device. No data is transmitted externally.

This policy applies to all versions of the app distributed through TestFlight and the App Store.

## Disclaimer

This tool is intended for **personal logbook reference only**.

It must not be relied upon for operational flight planning, navigation, or safety-critical decisions.

The results depend entirely on the data you enter. Incorrect or incomplete inputs may produce incorrect results. Always cross-check outputs and ensure they comply with applicable regulations before logging flight time.

Estimated Uncertainty is a confidence metric for the modeled day/night split. It is not suitable for operational use.

Edge cases, particularly at high latitudes or near twilight boundaries, may produce results that differ from expectation. If something looks unusual, double-check your inputs.

Airport data is sourced from OurAirports and may contain inaccuracies or omissions.
